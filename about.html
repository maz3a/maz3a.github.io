About me

Me

My course

Forensic Science isn't just CSI like you see on TV! There are a range of modules past fingerprinting and blood splatters.

I started my course in 2021, where I completed a science foundation year. Since 2022 I've been studying my main course and I'm now in year 3. Post this academic year, I'll be studying an intergrated masters. If you are interested click here to see what my course entails.

I've been a peer academic buddy, and my goal for this year is to become a student ambassador and a peer mentor. My goal is to complete my course then perhaps go into a nursing or doctor apprenticeship as it is something I've always dreamed of doing. Otherwise id like to work in scientific, financial or digital forensics.
